Split an real occupation number into alpha and beta space.

This routine does not perform any checks on the condition mod(nocc, 2) == 0 .eqv. mod(nuhf, 2) == 0 and will yield fractional occupations in case those condtions are not fullfilled. However, it will avoid creating negative occupation numbers.
